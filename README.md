## üîç Key Findings & Strategic Decisions

### Market Gap Identified
Existing UK trail running brands focus on either **extreme competition** (Inov-8, OMM) or **domestic community** running (Ronhill), leaving a clear opening for a brand with an **experiential, European-made** positioning.

### Competitive Landscape (Top Brands)

| Brand | Domain Authority | Instagram | Price Range | Positioning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Inov-8 | 58 | 159K | ¬£120‚Äì200 | Elite competition |
| Alpkit | 53 | 62.8K | ¬£70‚Äì140 | Price-conscious outdoor |
| Montane | 43 | 128K | ¬£140‚Äì220 | Premium expedition |
| Ronhill | 47 | 20.5K | ¬£80‚Äì160 | Domestic community |
| OMM | 45 | 20K | ¬£150‚Äì250 | Ultra-technical niche |

### Runforwind's Sweet Spot: **¬£120‚Äì180**
Positioned between Alpkit's accessible range and Montane/OMM's premium extreme tier ‚Äî justified by European manufacturing and experiential storytelling (not price discounting).

## üöÄ Go-to-Market Strategy Summary

### Phase 1 ‚Äî Foundation (Months 1‚Äì3)
- Adapt website for UK: GBP pricing, UK English copy, Klarna integration, Trustpilot, 30-day free returns
- Register for UK VAT (if >¬£85K threshold anticipated) and prepare Brexit documentation
- Launch 1‚Äì2 hero SKUs on Amazon.co.uk via FBA (50‚Äì100 units pilot)
- Begin B2B outreach to Run4It (DA 41 ‚Äî lowest barrier to entry)

### Phase 2 ‚Äî Growth (Months 4‚Äì8)
- Activate Google Shopping + Performance Max campaigns
- Launch Instagram & TikTok ads targeting UK trail running communities
- Pursue Runnersneed (DA 54) and Wiggle (DA 72) for distribution agreements
- Begin influencer/ambassador programme with UK trail running athletes

### Phase 3 ‚Äî Scale (Months 9‚Äì12)
- Optimise based on conversion data; double down on best-performing channel
- Expand Amazon catalogue to full range
- Launch B2B email sequences for independent running retailers
- Evaluate UK 3PL partnership to reduce per-order logistics costs

## ‚ö° Key Strategic Insights

**1. Klarna is a competitive advantage** ‚Äî No analysed brand prominently features buy-now-pay-later on their homepage, despite its popularity in UK for purchases over ¬£100.

**2. 30-day free returns are non-negotiable** ‚Äî This is the baseline expectation across all UK trail running e-commerce. Prepaid return labels must be included in packaging.

**3. Don't compete on discounts** ‚Äî Premium brands (Inov-8, Montane, OMM) deliberately avoid visible promotions. Runforwind should do the same to protect brand perception.

**4. Instagram > Facebook for this audience** ‚Äî OMM's ratio of 133 Facebook followers vs 20K Instagram followers makes the generational shift undeniable. Budget allocation should reflect this.

**5. Run4It is the B2B entry point** ‚Äî With a DA of 41 (lowest among multi-brand distributors), it represents the most accessible first partnership, before approaching Runnersneed or Wiggle.
